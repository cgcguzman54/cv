Matthew Kiichi Heafy (born January 26, 1986) is a Japanese-born American musician best known as the guitarist and lead vocalist for heafy metal band Trivium.

He was also the lead vocalist for the band Capharnaum along with Trivium's former producer Jason Suecof.
